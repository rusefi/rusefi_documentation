# ABS

While older ABS like your 1994 Miata work independently, modern highly integrated vehicles require vehicle specific CANbus integration for as-from-factory operation, and that's far from trivial. rusEFI does not provide out of box integration with any of the modern ABS vehicles - you are on your own, our job ends at giving you Lua scripting.
