## Supported
We hope that this board supports:
* M3.1 - M50
* M3.3 - M60 and Pre-1995 Euro M3
* M3.3.1 - M50tu and Pre-1995 USA M3

## Not supported
This board definitely does not support:

Siemens
* MS41.0 - E36 M52
* MS41.1 - E39 M52
* MS41.2 - E36 M3 USA Post 1995
