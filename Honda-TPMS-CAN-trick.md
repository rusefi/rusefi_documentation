# Honda TPMS CAN trick

The first generation of Honda K ECUs around 2002-2006 had CAN bus on the vehicle, (Honda called it "CAN-F" for "Fast") but no CAN pins on the OBD connector. Unhooking the TPMS module under the steering wheel is the way to access the CAN bus.
